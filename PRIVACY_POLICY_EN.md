## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Provide and Improve Services

- **Wardrobe Management**: Store and organize your clothing items locally on your device
- **AI Analysis**: Analyze item photos locally on your device using Apple Vision Framework to generate automatic descriptions, identify colors, materials, and styles
- **Outfit Generation**: Create personalized outfit combinations based on your preferences and wardrobe content
- **Background Removal**: Automatically remove backgrounds from item photos using local processing on your device via Apple Vision Framework
- **Laundry System**: Track items in laundry and send notifications when ready
- **Notifications**: Send personalized notifications with outfit suggestions and fashion inspiration

### 3.2 Authentication and Security

- Verify your identity and manage your account
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- Ensure app and data security

### 3.3 Communications

- Send push notifications related to outfits, laundry, and inspiration
- Respond to your support requests

### 3.4 Analysis and Improvements

- Analyze how you use the app to improve user experience
- Develop new features based on user preferences
- Fix bugs and technical issues

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### 4.1 Sharing with Third Parties

**IMPORTANT**: We do not use external servers for processing your personal data.

- **AI Analysis**: All AI analysis of your clothing items involves **local processing** on your device using Apple Vision Framework. No images are sent to external servers.

- **Background Removal**: Background removal is performed locally on your device using Apple Vision Framework.

- **Wardrobe Data**: Your clothing photos and all wardrobe data **ALWAYS remain on your local device**. They are NEVER uploaded to cloud servers or shared with third parties.

- **Synchronization**: Only information related to your account (email, display name, language, preferred styles, outfit preferences) and subscription status are synchronized with Firebase to allow you to access your data from multiple devices.

### 4.2 Sharing for Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to:
- Comply with legal obligations or court orders
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Protect user safety

### 4.3 Data Transfer in Case of Sale

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred to the new owner. We will notify you before any transfer.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage

- **Local Data**: Item photos, wardrobe details, saved outfits, and all information related to your clothing items are stored **LOCALLY on your device**. This data is NEVER synced to any cloud server.

- **Cloud Data**: Only the following information is saved on Firebase (Firestore):
  - Account information (email, display name)
  - Preferred language
  - Selected preferred styles
  - Outfit preferences (aggressiveness, formality, colors, comfort, trendiness, seasonality)
  - Premium subscription status (if active, expiration date, monthly counters)

- **User Backups**: You can create manual backups of your wardrobe (ZIP files) and store them wherever you prefer (iCloud, Google Drive, etc.).

### 5.2 Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

- **Local Storage**: All sensitive data (photos, preferences) remains on your device
- **Encryption**: Communications with Firebase are encrypted
- **Secure Authentication**: We use Firebase Authentication to manage credentials securely

**Note**: No system is completely secure. We recommend protecting your device with password/Face ID/Touch ID.

### 5.3 Data Retention

- **Active Account**: Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account.
- **Account Deletion**: You can delete your account and all associated data via Settings > Delete Account. Deletion is permanent:
  - Data on Firestore (only account information and subscription) will be deleted within 30 days
  - Local data on your device (photos and wardrobe) will be deleted immediately
- **Backup Data**: Manual backups you've created remain under your control and are not deleted by us.
